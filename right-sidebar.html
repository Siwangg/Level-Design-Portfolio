UE5 | WW2 Bunker

Developed within UE5 using the modelling and landscape tools as well as the Megascanns library for textures and assets. This scene was devloped over the space of a week.

Bunker was designed and created by me and used references from Vurdun, France as well as other WW1/WW2 bunkers

This level involves the player making their way towards the bunker, using a number of different routes and tactics including stealth and loud concepts.

Continued...

The goal of this level is to create a blockout for a bunker that was used in a past war. Ideally this will be in the form of a greybox arena within Unreal Engine 5.

Requirments for this scene

• The level has to be progress able.
• Level must have fragments for the player to interact with and think about when playing such as elevation changes
• Each area should have multiple escape routes, such as jumping from an elevation, escaping into another room … etc
• AI shootouts must work in each area that is playable
• A lot of obstacles for the player to hide behind for stealth
• Think of how a destructible environment could be played out here

Self-asked questions

• What kind of story is taking place?
• Where is the place? Any relics left behind to indicate what time period this is from?
• Does location meet requirements?
• I believe the location does meet the requirements and I will take inspiration from Verdun among other places for this especially in the back entrance of the bunker and landscape.
• When is the level taking place?
• I believe this level is taking place over 50-70 years after a war has taken place.
