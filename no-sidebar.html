About Me

Irish born level designer with over 1 years of experience working with Unity and Unreal Engine

I have been a level designer for just over a year now, working with both Unity and Unreal Engine 4 & 5. I began working with Unreal Engine 4 first then Unreal Engine 5 when it was released. The reason that I choose Unreal Engine is because it is the leading game/level development software available right now. The reason that this game is being made in Unity and not Unreal is because of the lower entry ceiling with Unity compared to Unreal. This was a big consideration for some of the programmers who have not been exposed to C++ and or Unreal Engine.

From the levels that I have made, there is a lot of care and detail that goes into them from early block outs to finished design decisions. I take great pride in the blockouts that I am making for my levels and will continually be working with members of my team to receive feedback and improvements that can be made.

I have a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science and Software Development. I am fluent in English as it is my first language. I have been working as a Compliace Analyst for 2 years. This role has taught be a lot about diligence and responsibilty. I am on a 2 man team in my job, myself and my manager so it is invaluble that I am able to be dependent and responsible.
