Untitled RPG Game

Currently in development using Unity 2022 and Blender

This is an isometric RPG based in the realm of Earth and the Gods. It is being made with Unity 2022.

The first level is based in the town that the hero visits when he has been released from the doctors office. Since this is based in the lands of the gods and earth there is small elements even know that the Gods are watching over everything.

I am responsible for everything that is seen within the level. Since we are a quite small team working on this, a handful of assets were made by other people. All of those assets were made via Blender.

This is an introductory level. It is meant to teach the player the core mechanics of the game and introduce them to the basic mobs that they will encounter along the way. This also includes a boss, Wurl, which can be seen below.
